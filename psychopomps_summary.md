### March 27, 2026

## The Concept

**PSYCHOPOMPS** (ψυχοπομπός) — Large weighted adult sleeping companions modeled after charismatic megafauna. The name references mythological guides of souls, reframed as "guides of souls to sleep."

- **Flagship product:** Greater oceanic manta ray — body-pillow scale, 15-30+ lbs
- **Future lineup:** Reticulated python (wraps around you), bearded vulture, and more
- **Construction:** Soft durable outer shell (minky or similar), removable/washable, internal baffled compartments filled with measured glass microbeads for weight distribution
- **Price point:** $250-350 per unit (premium tier)

## Target Market

- Adults dealing with loneliness, anxiety, and sleep issues
- Neurodivergent/autistic individuals who benefit from deep pressure stimulation (proprioceptive input)
- Emphatically intimate, not sexual — a weighted blanket reimagined as a creature you want to hold

### Mexico Production (Oaxaca)
- Friends on the ground in Oaxaca — third-largest state in Mexico for apparel manufacturing (3,567 registered economic units)
- Deep textile craftsmanship tradition, skilled workforce
- USMCA allows tariff-free goods movement if "yarn-forward" rule of origin is met (source fabric from US/Mexico, not China)
- Mexico is a net oil producer — less exposed to current energy crisis than trans-Pacific supply chains
- Infrastructure is less developed than northern Mexico but suitable for small-batch premium production
- **Plan:** Prototype locally, develop locked-in patterns and QC specs, then explore Oaxaca for scaled production
- Labor-intensive operations (filling, finishing) could begin in Mexico earlier
